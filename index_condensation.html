Condensation

In this facility, visualization of dropwise condensation on different surfaces at ambient conditions with controlled humidity can be performed. Thermocouples are used to measure the surface temperature and water collection rate is quantified using a scale. The surfaces are fabricated at NTNU NanoLab by using both wet and dry etching techniques.
