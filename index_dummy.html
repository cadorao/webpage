About us

The Thermal Two-Phase Flow Laboratory is located at the Department of Energy and Process Engineering (EPT), at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Trondheim, Norway.Our goal is to improve the understanding of the hydrodynamics and transport mechanisms of two-phase flow systems. Our research is mainly focused on gas-liquid and liquid-liquid systems, under adiabatic, boiling and condensing conditions. Activities in the group include micro/nanofabrication, experimental techniques and numerical methods.
